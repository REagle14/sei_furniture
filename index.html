About SEI

Our Business

Founded in 1989 by Carol Allin, Supplies Express Inc. (SEI) is a commercial furniture and design company based in Centreville, Virginia. SEI provides space planning, interior design, furniture selection , specification, and procurement services. Clients include GSA purchasers, federal, and local governments and others.

SEI has the capability to ship commercial furniture around the world, coordinate office moves and redesigns, and assemble and install furniture on site.

Carol is a seasoned design and procurement professional who owns several commercial furniture businesses along with her husband, Phil.
